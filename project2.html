cat's house

this is an interactive storybook made with stratch. it is still in its prototype phase.
there is a physical book that goes with it, and there are 3D folded elements. each part is handdrawn and handcut.
the book is meant to represent the two cat's house. there are a few rooms and when interacting with the specific
buttons we get to go to different parts of the house. make sure to hold the cat's hand to travel throughout their life!

plans for this project to go further is adding more locations as well as finalizing the overall construction
of the physical part.
