alien

alien is a p5.js prototype of a future generative art piece.
it is meant to represent the feeling of not fitting in but also embracing
those aspects. the background while have some fluidity to it in a randomized
way. the goal is to make the character animated and having it move in a natural way,
for now it is only in the 2d phase.
