running to the end

this is a platformer made in unreal engine. there are different mechanisms from falling platforms to moving ones,
as well as a mini maze. the goal in this game is to be able to make it through all the obstacles without failing.

this being my first UE made it a bit challenging as deciding how to make gameplay interesting without focusing
on art isn't something that is in my comfort zone. the game is meant to be casual and easy to play for anyone.
there are four levels, and they progressively get harder. coding some mechanics were tougher, but i ended up
replacing it with other mechanics.
